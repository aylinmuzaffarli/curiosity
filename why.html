Why this exists
This website is built by Aylin Muzaffarli, born in 2011.

Understanding the world down to its smallest scales gives me an incredible amount of satisfaction, excitement, and goosebumps.

This curiosity doesn’t let me sleep peacefully. I often wake up at night because a question appears in my head and I have to write it down. And it’s not only about sleep… I experience it everywhere. My starving brain never really stops.

In the morning, I start researching those questions. I search for answers, and I used to write everything into PDFs. Over time, those PDFs became massive and slow to navigate.

So I decided to build a system — something more structured, more beautiful, and faster to access.

This is how this website was born.

Hope you enjoy it.
If you want to reach out, the contact page is there.
